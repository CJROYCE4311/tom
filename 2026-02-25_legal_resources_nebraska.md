## Free Legal Assistance

### Legal Aid of Nebraska
- **Best option for Tom.** Serves all 93 Nebraska counties. Handles Social Security disability cases and Medicaid issues at no cost to qualifying individuals.
- At ~$1,300–$1,558/month gross income, Tom would very likely meet their income eligibility threshold.
- Can assist with both the SSDI claim and Medicaid (MIWD transfer, eligibility protection).
- **Website/Intake:** legalaidofnebraska.org/call-for-help
- **Coverage:** Statewide, including Lincoln

### Disability Rights Nebraska
- Nebraska's federally funded Protection & Advocacy (P&A) organization. Handles disability rights cases including SSA-related issues at no cost.
- Runs the **PABSS program** (Protection and Advocacy for Beneficiaries of Social Security) — technically limited to people already receiving SSI/SSDI cash benefits, so pending applicants like Tom may not qualify for PABSS specifically. However, the general P&A side can still assist with advocacy on the stalled claim.
- **Phone:** (402) 474-3183 or (800) 422-6691

### Nebraska State Bar Volunteer Lawyers Project
- Free pro bono legal program for low-income Nebraskans, based in Lincoln.
- **Phone:** (402) 986-6501
